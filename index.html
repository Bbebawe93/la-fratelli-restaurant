Welcome to La Fratelli Restaurant

La Fratelli is a family run restaurant since 1993. La Fratelli has been serving traditional Italian food in Italy, Netherlands and Belgium. Now it is the time for La Fratelli to take a new challenge and bring their authentic Italian food taste to London at sensible prices.

If you appreciate real Italian dinning experience - come over to La Fratelli the place where passion for quality food and high standards of customer service alongside with the unique Italian character

Here at La Fratelli, we pride ourselves on offering traditional dishes from all regions of Italy; our menu of antipasti, pizzas, pastas, risotti, salads and meat and fish secondi is prepared with the finest and freshest ingredients mostly sourced from Italy, and complimented by our excellent wine list.
